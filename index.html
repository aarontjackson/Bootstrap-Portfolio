About Me

Greetings all! My full name is Aaron Timothy Jackson. I was born in Clinton, MD and rised nearby in Waldorf, MD. I graduated from Florida A&M University with a Bachelor's degree in Policy Science and a Master's degree in Public Health. After graduating I regrettably asipred to go into politics, which fortunately did not work in my favor.

I decided to put my public health degree to use, and worked as the coordinator for Winston-Salem State University's Mobile Health program. This program provided underserving communities with access to free health services. Eventually, during my tenure I progressed to the program director. Although I loved helping people, I felt a bit unfillied and stagnant in my previous position. I decided to take a leap of faith, and enter the exciting and challenging field of coding.
